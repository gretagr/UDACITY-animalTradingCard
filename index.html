Wolf

Wolves are legendary because of their spine-tingling howl, which they use to communicate.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus
  • Average height: 80- 85cm
  • Mass: 30 - 80kg
  • Lifespan: 5 - 6 years

Wolf is the largest predator of the dog family. Wolves usually live in family groups, colloquially called packs.
